About

I've worked in Finance and VC, Biz Ops at a digital media startup (The Dodo, Seed to Series A), and Finance/Strategy at a healthtech startup (Monument, Seed to Series B). Most recently, I co-founded a B2B SaaS company (Build Health), which is proving to be the most difficult thing I've ever done. I have an MBA, not that I expect you to be impressed. I consider myself a "super generalist," but I have no illusions about being an expert in anything.

In my free time, which is increasingly dwindling, I do a little writing and a lot of thinking. I created this site to (A) house my musings, and (B) force myself to publicize them and open myself to critique.
